Our Kitchen Remodeling Process

A proven five-step approach that delivers exceptional results on time and within budget

1

Free In-Home Consultation

Your kitchen transformation begins with a comprehensive in-home assessment where we take detailed measurements, photograph your existing space, and discuss your vision, lifestyle needs, and style preferences. We'll explore your wish list-whether it's a larger island, better storage, or indoor-outdoor flow-and review your budget parameters. This no-obligation consultation gives us the information needed to create a proposal tailored specifically to your Orange County home. We'll also discuss realistic timelines, including current 8-12 week cabinet lead times, and answer all your questions about the remodeling process.

Timeline: 1-2 hours
2

Custom Kitchen Design & Planning

You'll visit our showroom to meet with your designer and lead contractor who'll collaborate on creating your custom kitchen plan. Using advanced 3D rendering software, we'll develop detailed visualizations showing exactly how your remodeled kitchen will look-from cabinet configurations to countertop selections and lighting placement. You'll see coastal-modern color palettes, material options, and layout alternatives. We'll refine the design until it perfectly reflects your vision. During this phase, we also handle permit applications for your city (Irvine, Anaheim, Costa Mesa, etc.), ensuring compliance with Orange County building codes before any work begins.

Timeline: 1-2 weeks
3

Material Selection & Approvals

With the design finalized, you'll select specific materials, finishes, and fixtures. Choose cabinet styles (trending white shakers or warm wood tones), countertop materials (quartz from Caesarstone, Silestone, or Cambria; granite with unique veining; or elegant marble), backsplash tiles in coastal blues or contemporary patterns, flooring options, hardware finishes (mixed metals are trending), and appliances. We'll walk you through our Anaheim cabinet manufacturing facility if you'd like to see craftsmanship firsthand. You'll receive a detailed contract outlining scope, materials, timeline, payment schedule, and our comprehensive warranty. We confirm cabinet production timelines and finalize the construction schedule.

Timeline: 1-2 weeks
4

Professional Construction & Installation

Your dedicated project manager coordinates all phases of construction. Demolition begins carefully, with teams protecting your home and maintaining clean worksites our clients consistently praise. Licensed electricians install dedicated 20-amp circuits and upgraded lighting. Plumbers relocate lines and update fixtures to code. If needed, we move walls to create open floor plans popular in Orange County homes. Our craftsmen install custom cabinets manufactured at our Anaheim facility, followed by precise countertop fabrication and installation with waterfall edges or custom profiles. We complete flooring installation, stunning backsplash tile work, and integrate all appliances. Throughout construction, your project manager provides regular updates and addresses any questions.

Timeline: 6-12 weeks (including 8-12 week cabinet production)
5

Final Walkthrough & Satisfaction Check

Before we consider the project complete, we conduct a detailed final walkthrough together. We'll inspect every element-cabinet alignment, countertop seams, tile grout lines, appliance operation, lighting function, and hardware installation. We demonstrate how to care for your new quartz countertops, maintain cabinet finishes, and operate any smart technology we've integrated. You'll receive all warranty information, care instructions, and our contact details for any future questions. We ensure your complete satisfaction, addressing any final touches needed. Only when you're thrilled with your transformed kitchen do we request final payment. Many clients tell us the final reveal exceeds their expectations.

Timeline: 1-2 hours

Please Note: Current custom cabinet lead times in Orange County are 8-12 weeks. We recommend adding 20% to your budget for unexpected issues that can arise in older homes. Timeline estimates include design, permitting, cabinet manufacturing, and construction phases. Your project manager will provide specific timelines based on your project scope during the planning phase.

Orange County Kitchen Design Trends 2024-2026

Our portfolio reflects the evolving aesthetic preferences of Orange County homeowners. In 2024, we witnessed the strong return of warm tones and natural wood elements in kitchens throughout Irvine, Newport Beach, and coastal communities. The warmth of wood provides perfect contrast to gorgeous quartz countertops while adding organic texture and sophistication. This trend continues gaining momentum into 2026.

Color has emerged stronger than ever in 2026, though not with the in-your-face saturation of previous decades. Instead, new hues debut thoughtfully in contrasting upper or lower cabinets, creating visual interest without overwhelming spaces. We're seeing deep navy blues paired with white uppers, sage greens balanced with natural wood, and even sophisticated charcoals creating dramatic contemporary statements. Backsplash areas have transformed into palettes for colorful tiles and exquisite patterns-from cool blues and greens paying homage to Orange County's beautiful seaside to tans, rusts, and browns pulling inspiration from mountain landscapes.

The coastal-modern aesthetic dominates our recent Orange County projects from Laguna Beach's older homes to Irvine's newer developments. This style embraces light, airy colors including soft blues, seafoam greens, and crisp whites. Sea glass tile backsplashes and light-colored countertops create tranquil, beach-inspired ambiance that resonates with the California coastal lifestyle. These kitchens feel open, bright, and connected to the beautiful Orange County environment.

Mixing metals has become standard practice rather than design risk. We're combining brass, chrome, and matte black finishes in single kitchens with stunning results. Hardware no longer needs to match between plumbing fixtures and cabinet pulls-this freedom allows more personality and depth. Gold and brass accents particularly shine against white cabinets and quartz countertops, adding warmth and sophistication.

The pivot toward Scandinavian design continues strengthening in 2026, moving away from Asian aesthetics that dominated earlier years. This minimalist approach emphasizes simplicity, clean lines, and clutter-free environments. Sleek handleless cabinets, integrated appliances that disappear into cabinetry, and streamlined profiles create calm, organized spaces that Orange County homeowners appreciate for both beauty and functionality.

Several of our luxury projects have been featured in Luxe Magazine, showcasing quiet luxury with custom white oak cabinetry, bespoke millwork, and curated high-end finishes. These magazine-worthy kitchens demonstrate that true luxury doesn't shout-it whispers through impeccable craftsmanship, thoughtful material selections, and details that reveal themselves over time.
